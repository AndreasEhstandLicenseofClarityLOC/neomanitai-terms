# AUGMANITAI Compendium: 1000 Terms for Human-AI Interaction

## Dataset Description

The AUGMANITAI Compendium is a research-grade terminology system containing 1000 precisely defined terms describing observable phenomena in human-AI interaction. Each term captures a specific pattern, behavior, or experience that emerges when humans work with AI systems in everyday contexts.

### Dataset Summary

- **1000 terms** with bilingual definitions (EN/DE)
- **7 thematic bands**: Foundation, Claude Expansion, Gemini Expansion, Cultural/Global, Society/Ethics/Governance, Technical/Agentic, Synthesis
- **2901 cross-references** between terms
- **Ethical distinctions** for every term
- **Published under CC BY-NC-ND 4.0**

### Languages
English, German

### Use Cases
- Training AI systems to recognize and discuss human-AI interaction patterns
- Building terminology-aware chatbots and writing assistants
- Academic research on AI literacy and human-AI collaboration
- Creating educational materials about AI interaction phenomena

## Dataset Structure

### Data Fields

- `term_id`: Canonical identifier (e.g., AUG-0001)
- `english_name`: Term name in English
- `german_name`: Term name in German
- `definition_en`: Full definition in English
- `definition_de`: Full definition in German
- `distinction_en`: Ethical/conceptual distinction in English
- `distinction_de`: Ethical/conceptual distinction in German
- `band`: Thematic band (1-7)
- `relations`: Cross-references to related terms
- `short_definition`: Quotable short definition (<180 chars)
