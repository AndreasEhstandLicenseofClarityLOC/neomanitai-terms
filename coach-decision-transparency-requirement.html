📖Definition

Coaches who explain why they accept or decline an algorithm suggestion. Old "because I said so" no longer works.

📖Definition (DE)

Athletes expect coaches to explain their reasoning when accepting or rejecting an algorithm's recommendation. The old appeal to authority—"because I said so"—no longer works as justification.

What makes this phenomenon observable?
EN

This term describes a pattern that emerges across multiple contexts and observers. It is not prescriptive — there is no recommendation about whether one does or does not experience or exhibit this phenomenon. It is a documented observation of how human-AI interaction manifests.

DE

How does this differ from related concepts?
EN

This phenomenon describes a specific intersection of human experience and AI system behavior. It is distinct from general technology adoption, human-computer interaction theory, or AI capability measurement. The term isolates a particular observable pattern.
