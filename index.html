Open-access research lexicon. This project does not encourage, promote, or glorify intensive AI use. It creates neutral language for phenomena that occur when humans interact with AI — both positive experiences and concerning patterns are made equally describable. Without words, there can be no reflection, no critical discourse, and no informed decision-making. Every term is a neutral observation. ISO 704 inspired. CC BY-NC-ND 4.0.

Scientific context & methodology

NEOMANITAI systematically names and catalogs human experiences that occur during interaction with artificial intelligence — experiences that have always existed but lacked precise terminology. The lexicon covers the full spectrum: from moments of creative joy to patterns of dependency, from productive collaboration to critical skill changes. The purpose is enabling scientific discourse: phenomena can only be studied, compared, and discussed once precise words exist for them.

All terms are formulated in impersonal, third-person language without value judgments. The project follows ISO 704/1087/30042 terminology standards and is published open-access with DOI registration.
