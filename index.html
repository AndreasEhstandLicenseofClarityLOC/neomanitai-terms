About this project — Scientific context & scope

NEOMANITAI is a descriptive, exploratory, phenomenological research project. It systematically names and catalogs human experiences that occur during interaction with artificial intelligence — experiences that have always existed but lacked precise terminology.

This work is purely descriptive. It does not recommend, encourage, or discourage the use of AI systems. It does not provide guidance, therapy, advice, or instructions of any kind. Every term documents an observable phenomenon — nothing more.

The purpose is enabling scientific discourse: phenomena can only be studied, compared, and discussed across disciplines and cultures once precise words exist for them. Without vocabulary, there is no shared understanding. This compendium provides that vocabulary.

All terms are neutral observations, formulated in impersonal, third-person language without value judgments or calls to action. The project follows ISO 704/1087/30042 terminology standards and is published open-access under CC BY-NC-ND 4.0 with DOI registration.
