⚡ Context · Why terminology matters

Unnamed = hard to share. Millions interact with AI daily and experience phenomena that lack clear names. Shared terminology enables precise comparison, discussion, and systematic research across languages and cultures.

Language creates clarity. When something can be named precisely, it can be understood precisely. A single term transforms a vague experience into a recognizable, describable pattern — across disciplines, across borders.

No vocabulary, no science. Scientific fields develop terminology to organize knowledge. Human-AI interaction currently lacks widely adopted vocabulary. This compendium provides systematic terminology — ISO 704/1087/30042 inspired, DOI-published, open access.

Recognition enables discussion. Named phenomena can be identified, discussed, and compared systematically. What is observable becomes measurable.
