📖Definition

Chronic elevation of baseline muscle tension during AI work — not in a specific location like the neck (Sustained-Strain Response) but as a generalized increase in muscular tonus across the body. The user sits in a state of physical readiness that has no appropriate physical outlet. Over time, this elevated tone produces secondary symptoms: jaw clenching, shoulder elevation, forearm tightness. The body is perpetually braced for something that never arrives in physical form.

Mit der Zeit produziert dieser erhöhte Tonus Sekundärsymptome: Kieferpressen, Schultererhöhung, Unterarmspannung. Der Körper ist permanent für etwas gewappnet, das niemals in physischer Form eintrifft.
